Programming Contests
  • Sphere Online Judge: An exhaustive collection of problems. A few problems written by me: 1, 2, 3
  • Topcoder: Fun and addictive. One of my goals from my list of ridiculously-unrealistic-and-useless goals was to go past a rating of 2200.  But 2050 was the best I could do. Well, there's still time. Another of my ridiculously-unrealistic-and-useless goal was to qualify for TCO finals in Vegas which I managed in 2008 in Marathon track. I think I slept for less than 5 hours for 10 days straight during the final online round.  Some of my favorite topcoders from India (in no particular order): myprasanna, syco, innocentboy, humblefool, konqueror  -- It takes both hardwork and talent.
  • Google Code Jam: I qualified for Google Code Jam India 2006 finals purely by chance and thats when I started programming contests. I think I have learned more from these contests than from any single course. Problems in Google Code Jams tend to be fresh, unlike other contests.
  • Codechef: Some very good problem setters, cash prizes and no time!
